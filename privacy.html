The Mental Offload ("we," "us," or "our") operates the mobile applications YahtzEats and BoopTime (the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Apps.

1. Who we are

The Mental Offload is the data controller for personal information processed through the Apps. Contact: thementaloffload@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and authentication identifiers when you create an account.
  • App data you provide: meal preferences, saved menus, favorites, custom recipes (YahtzEats), and similar content you save in the Apps.
  • Usage and technical data: device type, app version, and diagnostic logs needed to operate and secure the service.
  • Communications: messages you send us (for example, support or account deletion requests).

3. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Apps
  • Authenticate you and enforce access controls
  • Generate menus and personalized features you request
  • Respond to support and legal requests
  • Protect against abuse, fraud, and security incidents

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service providers that help us run the Apps (for example, cloud hosting, authentication, and database services), under contractual obligations to protect your data
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Data storage and security

Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure. Traffic between the Apps and our servers uses encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we use reasonable safeguards appropriate to the service.

6. Retention

We keep your information while your account is active and as needed to provide the Apps. When you request account deletion, we delete or anonymize associated data as described on our Delete Account & Data page, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Access or update certain information in the Apps where available
  • Request account deletion — see Delete Account & Data
  • Contact us at thementaloffload@gmail.com with privacy questions

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws.

8. Children

The Apps are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

9. International users

Your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our providers operate.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
