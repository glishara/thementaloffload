Overview

The Mental Offload ("we", "our") and BoopTime ("the app") are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it. For YahtzEats, see the YahtzEats Privacy Policy.

Information we collect

We do not store any data off of your device.

BoopTime keeps the information you enter on your phone or tablet. We do not operate cloud accounts, sync servers, or remote databases for BoopTime.

Children's privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Removing your data

To remove BoopTime data, uninstall the app or clear the app's storage in your device settings. There is no server account to delete. If you email us, we cannot delete data we do not hold.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
