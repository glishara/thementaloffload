BoopTime

Track your life; boop the snoot.

BoopTime is a simple, flexible "anything tracker" that lets you log anything you want with a single tap. Whether you're monitoring habits, pet behaviors, chores, productivity, water intake, or wins and setbacks, the power to track them is literally at your fingertips.

Every tracked item is represented by a colorful little "boop snoot"—a tiny nose that longs for booping and is deeply satisfying to tap.

  • Download on the App Store (iOS)
  • Download for Android

Features

  • One-tap tracking for anything
  • Create up to fifteen custom trackers ("snoots")
  • Optional intensity or rating levels (1–5)
  • Add notes to any boop
  • Delete or correct entries anytime
  • Calendar view for watching patterns over time
  • List view for detailed history
  • Compare up to four trackers side-by-side in the calendar
  • Colorful, playful interface designed to make tracking feel rewarding instead of stressful
  • Local data storage—BoopTime never accesses or stores any information on our servers

Perfect for tracking

  • Habits and routines
  • Exercise and fitness
  • Productivity
  • Pet care
  • Parenting events
  • Nutrition and hydration
  • Custom personal goals

BoopTime is built for people who need a low-friction tracker. It provides basic functionality with a very low learning curve and no complicating dashboards.
